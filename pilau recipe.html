ingredients

serving for 8 people

PILAU MASALA

    2 teaspoons groung cumin
    1 tablespoon paprika
    1 teaspoon groung cardamon
    1 teaspoon black pepper
    1/4 teaspoon ground cloves

PILAU

    1/4 cup vegetable oil
    2 medium red onions,thinly sliced
    4 cloves garlic,minced
    2 tablespoons ginger,minced
    1 serrano chile,finely chopped
    1 tablespoon pilau masala
    2 beef stock cubes
    1 cinnamon stick
    1/4 cup of fresh cilantro(10g) roughly chopped
    1lb potato(500g),waxy,peeled and cut into meduim size chunks
    4 cups water(960ml)
    2 cups basmati rice(400g)

PROCEDURE

    Make the pilau masala:In a medium bowl ,combine the cumin,paprika,cardamom,black pepper and cloves.set
    Heat a large pot over medium-high heat.Add the oil and red onion and saute for 10-15minutes,until dark brown
    Add the garlic,ginger,serrano chile,pile masala,beef stock cubes,cinnamon stick,and cilantro.Cook for 1-2minutes,until fragrant.
    Add the beef and cook until browned,about 8 minutes
    Add the tomatoes and cook 4-5minutes,until the tomatoes have released their liquid
    Add the potatoes and the water.Bring to a boil and cook for 10minutes
    Add the rice,stir,and cover.Reduce the heat to medium-low and cook for 20minutes,until the rice is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.
